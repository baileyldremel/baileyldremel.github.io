Playfully Rigid

Playfully Rigid is a collection of articles written by members of Design Observer that discuss the history of publication design.


This project was part of a university assignment for Intro to Publication Design. As part of the assignment, we were tasked to create a 16 page tabloid featuring 7 design observer articles using the skills we aquired throughout the semester.
