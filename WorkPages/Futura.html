Futura Type Sampler

To show off the best characteristics of a typeface the best place to do that is within a sampler.


This project was created for the 'Typography in Design' class at RMIT University. Futura is one of my favourite typefaces and I have used it quite a bit throughout the years. For this assignment, I wanted to challenge myself and show Futura off in a different light.

Project Information

Project for Typography in Design at RMIT University.

- Publication Design

- Graphic Design


Software used:

Adobe Illustrator and InDesign
