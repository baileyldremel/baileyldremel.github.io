Over the Rainbow - Intersect

Over the Rainbow started as an idea in 2019 to create a safe space for LGBTQIA+ people in the south east of Melbourne. After a tough 2020 that had put OtR's plans to a hault, they are coming back in 2021 with a new identity.

About OtR


Over the Rainbow (also known as OtR in short) is a safe space that is facilitated and curated by queer artists/creatives wanting to connect to chitchat about all things queer.
