Allied Mental Health

Mental health is something that is often stigmatised. Allied Mental Health wants to break that barrier.


This was a project I did for 'Image and Identity' at RMIT University. The task was to take a issue (such as mental health) and design something that targets the audience of your chosen issue. Mental Health is a massive issue within the community and I've struggled with the stigma of mental health so I chose to create something that would break down that stigma.

Project Information

Project for Image and Identity at RMIT University.

- Logo Design

- Graphic Design


Software used:

Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop
